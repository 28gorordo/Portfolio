Twine Story

Reflection: This story focusses on the ethics of when robots should be used, and how far is too far when it comes to using robots even if it is for the greater good. The story reflects asimovs first law, "A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm" and second law, "A robot must obey orders given it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law". Is invading a humans privacy harm? Is a robot protecting a human when it is causing the human distress? Is it okay for a robot to disobey the second law to proform the first law? These are some of the themes explored in this short, interactive, story.
